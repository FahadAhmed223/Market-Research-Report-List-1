<p><p>The Foot Joint Orthosis System market is categorized into two main types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted devices. </p><p>Inlay Type orthoses are designed to be placed inside footwear, providing support and alignment primarily through the insoles. They enhance comfort and flexibility for users with mild foot deformities. </p><p>Externally Assisted orthoses, on the other hand, are external devices that offer more substantial structural support. They often encompass various supportive elements such as straps and braces, addressing more severe foot conditions effectively.</p></p>
