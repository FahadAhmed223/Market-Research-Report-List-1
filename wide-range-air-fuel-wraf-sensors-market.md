<p><p>Wide Range Air Fuel (WRAF) sensors are categorized into three main types: Battery Type, Critical Current Type, and Pump Battery Type. </p><p>Battery Type sensors utilize a built-in battery to operate, ensuring consistent performance. Critical Current Type sensors rely on precise current measurements to detect air-fuel ratios, enhancing accuracy. Pump Battery Type sensors incorporate a pumping mechanism powered by a battery, allowing for dynamic adjustments to air-fuel mixtures. Each type serves unique applications in optimizing engine performance and emissions control in various automotive systems.</p></p>
