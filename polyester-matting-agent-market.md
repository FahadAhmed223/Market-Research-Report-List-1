<p><p>The Polyester Matting Agent market primarily comprises two types: gel and powder. Gel matting agents are preferred for water-based formulations, providing a smooth texture and enhanced application properties. They offer ease of use and uniform dispersion in various mediums. Powder matting agents, on the other hand, are suitable for solvent-based systems, delivering a matte finish with effective light scattering. Both types cater to diverse industries, including paints, coatings, and plastics, enhancing product performance and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
