<p><p>The lock washer market is categorized into various types based on their design and application. External star washers possess protruding teeth that dig into the assembly surface, providing superior locking. Split washers, or spring washers, create tension and resist rotation, making them versatile. Other types include flat and conical washers, each offering unique benefits for specific applications in automotive, construction, and machinery sectors.</p></p>
