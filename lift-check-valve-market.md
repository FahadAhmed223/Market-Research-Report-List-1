<p><ul><li>Vertical Check Valve</li><li>Horizontal Check Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lift Check Valve market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal check valves. Vertical check valves are oriented such that the fluid flow is directed upwards, allowing gravity to assist in sealing, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Horizontal check valves, on the other hand, are placed parallel to the flow direction, often used in pipelines where space is limited. Both types are crucial for preventing backflow and ensuring efficient fluid management in various industrial applications.</p></p>
