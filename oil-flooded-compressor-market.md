<p><p>The oil-flooded compressor market primarily includes two types: rotary screw compressors and reciprocating compressors. Rotary screw compressors utilize two interlocking helical screws to compress air, offering continuous operation and higher efficiency. They are widely used in industrial applications. In contrast, reciprocating compressors use a piston mechanism to compress air in cycles, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and smaller air demands. Both types are essential for various industries, including manufacturing, oil and gas, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
