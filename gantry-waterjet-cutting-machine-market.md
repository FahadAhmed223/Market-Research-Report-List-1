<p><strong>The Gantry Waterjet Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gantry Waterjet Cutting Machine market is divided into two main types: Pressurized Water and Abrasive Mixture. Pressurized Water systems utilize high-pressure streams of water to cut softer materials like foam or rubber. In contrast, Abrasive Mixture machines incorporate abrasive materials, such as garnet, into the water stream, enabling them to cut through tougher materials like metal, stone, and glass. Both methods are valued for their precision and versatility, catering to a diverse range of industrial applications.</p></p>
