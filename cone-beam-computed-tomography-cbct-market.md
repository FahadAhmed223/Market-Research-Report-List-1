<p><p>Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) is an advanced imaging technique that provides 3D visualization of dental and maxillofacial structures with high precision. Unlike traditional CT, CBCT uses a cone-shaped X-ray beam to capture images, resulting in lower radiation doses and rapid acquisition times. This technology is widely employed in dental practices for applications such as implant planning, orthodontics, and treatment of craniofacial anomalies.</p><p>The Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include increasing demand for non-invasive imaging techniques, rising prevalence of dental disorders, and advancements in imaging technology. <p><p>Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) is an advanced imaging technique that generates three-dimensional images of dental and maxillofacial structures. The market for CBCT can be segmented based on detection technologies, primarily into Image Intensifier Detectors and Flat-Panel Imager Detectors. Image Intensifier Detectors convert X-ray photons into visible light, producing enhanced images with lower radiation doses. In contrast, Flat-Panel Imager Detectors utilize digital sensors for direct image capture, offering improved image quality, faster processing times, and greater convenience in clinical settings.</p></p>
