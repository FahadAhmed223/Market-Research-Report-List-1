<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Welding Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stick Electrodes</li><li>Solid Wires</li><li>Flux Cored Wires</li><li>SAW Wires & Fluxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding consumables market includes various types used in different welding processes. **Stick electrodes** are coated metal rods used in manual arc welding, offering versatility and ease of use. **Solid wires** are uncoated wires used in MIG welding, providing clean and efficient welds. **Flux-cored wires** contain a flux core, enabling better penetration and suitability for outdoor welding. **SAW wires and fluxes** are used in submerged arc welding, offering high deposition rates and deep penetration for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific welding needs and environments.</p></p>
