<p><p>The Solar Keratoses Treatment Market comprises three main types: topical medications, photodynamic therapy, and destructive treatments. Topical medications, such as creams and gels, help reduce lesions by promoting skin regeneration. Photodynamic therapy utilizes light-sensitive agents activated by specific wavelengths of light to destroy abnormal cells. Destructive treatments include cryotherapy, electrosurgery, and laser therapy, which physically eliminate the keratoses. Each treatment option varies in approach and efficacy, catering to different patient needs and the severity of solar keratosis.</p></p>
