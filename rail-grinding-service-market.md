<p><p>Rail grinding services are essential for maintaining railway track integrity and enhancing safety. The market can be segmented into two primary types: switch grinding and mainline grinding. </p><p>Switch grinding focuses on maintaining the crucial points and crossings where trains change tracks, ensuring smooth operation and reducing wear. In contrast, mainline grinding addresses the longer stretches of track utilized by high-speed trains. Both services enhance rail longevity, improve ride quality, and reduce maintenance costs, thereby supporting efficient rail operations.</p></p>
