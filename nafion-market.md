Nafion membranes are primarily used in fuel cells and electrolyzers, providing ionic conductivity while acting as a barrier to gases. Nafion dispersions are aqueous solutions utilized in various applications like coatings and membranes, enhancing adhesion and performance. Nafion resins serve as ion-exchange materials in chemical processes and water treatment. Together, these products are essential in energy, environmental, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
