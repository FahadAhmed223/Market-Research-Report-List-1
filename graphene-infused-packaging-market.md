<p><p>The graphene-infused packaging market is categorized into single-layer and multiple-layer types. Single-layer graphene packaging utilizes a single, homogeneous layer of graphene to enhance barrier properties, mechanical strength, and thermal stability, making it suitable for certain food and pharmaceutical applications. In contrast, multiple-layer packaging combines graphene with other materials, creating a composite that offers improved protection, flexibility, and durability. This type is often used for a wider range of products, leveraging the synergistic effects of different materials to optimize performance.</p></p>
