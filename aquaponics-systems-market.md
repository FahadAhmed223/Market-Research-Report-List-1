<p><p>Aquaponics systems integrate aquaculture and hydroponics, creating a symbiotic environment for fish and plants. The Nutrient Film Technique (NFT) utilizes a thin film of nutrient-rich water flowing over plant roots, promoting efficient nutrient uptake. Deep Water Culture (DWC) suspends plant roots in oxygenated nutrient solutions, enabling rapid growth. Media-filled grow beds combine soil-like media to support plants and filter water, providing stability and beneficial bacteria for nutrient conversion. Each system caters to diverse market needs, from home gardeners to commercial farms.</p></p>
