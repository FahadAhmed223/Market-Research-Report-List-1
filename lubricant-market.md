<p><p>The lubricant market is segmented into three main types: semi-synthetic, synthetic, and mineral lubricants. Semi-synthetic lubricants combine mineral oil with synthetic additives, offering enhanced performance and better temperature stability. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for superior characteristics, providing excellent protection and efficiency, particularly in extreme conditions. Mineral lubricants are derived from refining crude oil and are the most traditional type, commonly used for general applications. Each type serves specific needs based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
