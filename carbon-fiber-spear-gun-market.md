<p><p>The carbon fiber spear gun market features two main types: pneumatic guns and sling guns. Pneumatic guns utilize pressurized air for propulsion, offering high accuracy and power, making them ideal for deep-sea fishing. In contrast, sling guns rely on elastic bands for launching spears, providing a simple and lightweight option suitable for shallow waters. Both types cater to different user preferences and environments, with carbon fiber construction enhancing durability and performance across various fishing conditions.</p></p>
