<p><p>The veterinary RFID readers market is segmented into fixed and handheld RFID readers. Fixed RFID readers are stationed in animal entry points, like gates or examination rooms, for continuous monitoring and quick identification of animals. They help streamline processes in facilities. Handheld RFID readers, on the other hand, are portable devices used by veterinary staff for on-the-spot tracking, documentation, and health assessments. Both types enhance animal management, improve care efficiency, and ensure accurate data collection in veterinary practices.</p></p>
