<p><p>The Jet Dispensing Valve market is categorized into two main types: Contact Jet Valves and Non-contact Jet Valves. Contact Jet Valves operate by making direct contact with the substrate, allowing precise material deposition, ideal for thicker fluids. In contrast, Non-contact Jet Valves release materials without physical contact, facilitating higher-speed applications and minimizing contamination risks. Both types serve diverse industries, including electronics and automotive, by enhancing efficiency and precision in dispensing processes.</p></p>
