<p><strong>The Composite Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite plate market comprises two primary types: explosion bonded and roll bonded plates. Explosion bonded plates are created through a high-energy process where two metals are rapidly joined, resulting in strong, durable materials ideal for applications requiring high resistance to corrosion and extreme conditions. Roll bonded plates involve a mechanical process where two or more metals are pressed together at high temperatures, enabling efficient production and customization. Both types serve diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction, emphasizing their versatility and performance.</p></p>
