<p><p>The Hypersensitive Troponin I Test Kit market comprises various testing methods, notably Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent markers and time-resolved detection to enhance sensitivity, allowing for precise measurement of troponin levels in cardiac patients. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent reactions to quantify troponin, providing rapid results with high specificity. Both methods are crucial in diagnosing myocardial infarctions and assessing cardiac health, driving advancements in point-of-care testing and laboratory diagnostics.</p></p>
