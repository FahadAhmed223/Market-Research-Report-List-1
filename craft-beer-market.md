<p><strong>The Craft Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ales</li><li>Lagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The craft beer market features two primary types: ales and lagers. Ales are characterized by their top-fermenting yeast, resulting in a wide range of flavors and aromas, often fruity or spicy. They typically have a shorter fermentation process. Lagers, on the other hand, utilize bottom-fermenting yeast, producing a cleaner, crisper taste, and are fermented at cooler temperatures over a longer period. Both categories attract diverse consumer preferences, contributing to the growing popularity of craft breweries and innovation in brewing styles.</p></p>
