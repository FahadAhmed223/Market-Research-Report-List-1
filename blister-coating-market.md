<p><p>Blister coating refers to a process that applies protective and decorative coatings to products, typically pharmaceutical tablets and capsules. The market is divided into solvent-based and water-based categories. Solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering strong adhesion and durability, but may pose environmental and health concerns due to volatile organic compounds. In contrast, water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, making them more eco-friendly and safer, while still providing good performance in terms of adhesion and appearance.</p></p>
