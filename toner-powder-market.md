<p><p>The toner powder market encompasses two main types: physical toner and chemical toner. Physical toner consists of finely ground particles typically used in laser printing and photocopying to produce images and text. This type is known for its efficiency and quality. In contrast, chemical toner is made through chemical processes, offering finer particles and improved adhesion, resulting in better print quality and reduced waste. Both types serve specific applications in printing technology, catering to various consumer needs.</p></p>
