<p><p>The Aircraft Arresting System market includes various types designed for controlling aircraft landings. Net barriers utilize netting to capture aircraft, while cable systems employ steel cables to decelerate them effectively. Mobile Aircraft Arresting Systems (MAAS) offer flexibility for different runway conditions. The Engineered Material Arresting System (EMAS) uses specially designed materials to safely decelerate aircraft that overrun runways. Lastly, aircraft carrier arresting systems are tailored for naval operations, using cables and hydraulic mechanisms to rapidly stop landing aircraft on carriers.</p></p>
