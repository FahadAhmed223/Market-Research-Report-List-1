<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Line Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The line isolator market is classified into three main types based on their operational configuration: bus side, line side, and transfer bus side. </p><p>Bus side isolators are installed between the bus and the transformer, ensuring safety during maintenance. Line side isolators connect directly to the incoming power line, offering system protection and control. Transfer bus side isolators facilitate switching between two buses for redundancy, maintaining power supply continuity. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing system reliability and operational efficiency.</p></p>
