<p><p>Laser patterning machines are categorized into several types based on the laser technology they employ. YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) laser type is known for its precision and efficiency in cutting and engraving materials, making it ideal for metal and hard substrates. CO2 laser type utilizes carbon dioxide gas to produce high-quality engravings on non-metal materials like wood, glass, and acrylic. Other types may include fiber lasers and excimer lasers, each suited for specific applications and materials within various industries.</p></p>
