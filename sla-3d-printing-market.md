<p><p>The SLA 3D printing market comprises three primary segments: metal printing, plastics printing, and ceramics printing. Metal printing utilizes photopolymerization to create intricate metal components, ideal for industries like aerospace and automotive. Plastics printing, the most common SLA application, produces lightweight, durable prototypes and end-use parts from various resin types. Ceramics printing leverages SLA technology to craft complex ceramic items, often used for artistic, dental, and engineering applications, offering high precision and diverse material properties.</p></p>
