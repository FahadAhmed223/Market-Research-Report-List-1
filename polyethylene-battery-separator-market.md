<p><p>Polyethylene battery separators are thin, porous films made from polyethylene that are essential components in lithium-ion batteries. Their high thermal stability, chemical resistance, and excellent mechanical properties contribute to the overall performance and safety of batteries.</p><p>The market for polyethylene battery separators is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and portable electronic devices.
