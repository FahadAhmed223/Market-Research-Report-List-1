<p><p>The dispersive mirror market is characterized by advanced optical components, primarily including Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) mirrors and chirped mirrors. GTI mirrors utilize multiple thin-film coatings to enhance reflectivity while maintaining a narrow bandwidth, ideal for laser applications. Chirped mirrors, on the other hand, are designed with varying thicknesses to compensate for different wavelengths, enabling pulse compression in femtosecond lasers. Both types are crucial in fields like telecommunications, materials processing, and scientific research, driving innovation and demand in the optics industry.</p></p>
