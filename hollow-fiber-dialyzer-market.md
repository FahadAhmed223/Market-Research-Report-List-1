<p><strong>The Hollow Fiber Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hollow fiber dialyzer market comprises two primary types: low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes are designed for patients with stable renal function, allowing for the gradual removal of small solutes and waste products. In contrast, high flux membranes facilitate greater solute clearance by allowing larger molecules to pass through, making them suitable for patients requiring more intensive dialysis treatments. Each type serves distinct patient needs, influencing choice based on medical conditions and treatment goals.</p></p>
