<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection Device Market comprises several types, including self-restoring fuses, thermal fuses, and other variations. Self-restoring fuses are designed to reset after a discharge event, offering continuous protection without replacement. Thermal fuses provide protection by melting under excessive heat, ceasing current flow. Other market types may include diodes, varistors, and polymer-based devices, each tailored to specific applications and requirements, ensuring effective safeguarding against ESD in electronic components and systems.</p></p>
