<p><p>An Optical Parametric Oscillator (OPO) is a device that converts a pump beam into two lower-energy signal and idler beams through a nonlinear optical process. The OPO market is categorized into two primary types: Single Resonance and Double Resonance. Single Resonance OPOs operate with resonance in the signal or idler cavity, enhancing efficiency, while Double Resonance OPOs achieve resonance in both cavities, resulting in broader tuning capabilities and improved output power, catering to diverse applications in scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
