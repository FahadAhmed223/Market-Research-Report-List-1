<p><p>Water-based lithium-ion battery binders are crucial for manufacturing both anodes and cathodes in lithium-ion batteries. Anode binders, typically made from materials like carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC), provide adhesion and flexibility to the anode materials, facilitating charge transfer. In contrast, cathode binders, often utilizing materials such as polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), enhance the structural integrity and conductivity of cathodes. The shift to water-based options reduces harmful solvents, making production processes more environmentally friendly while maintaining performance and efficiency in energy storage applications.</p></p>
