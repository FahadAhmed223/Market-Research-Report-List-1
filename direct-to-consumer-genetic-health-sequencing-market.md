<p><p>The direct-to-consumer genetic health sequencing market encompasses various types, including diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening involves analyzing genetic information to identify potential health risks and inherited conditions. PGD is used by prospective parents to assess embryos for genetic disorders before implantation during in vitro fertilization, ensuring healthier genetic outcomes. Relationship testing evaluates genetic connections, such as paternity or familial bonds, providing insights into ancestry and biological relationships. Each type enhances informed decision-making about health and relationships.</p></p>
