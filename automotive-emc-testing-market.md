<p><strong>The Automotive EMC Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Current Injection (BCI)</li><li>Conducted Emissions (CE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI)</li><li>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)</li><li>Radiated Emissions (RE)</li><li>Radiated Immunity (RI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive EMC Testing Market focuses on ensuring vehicle components and systems comply with electromagnetic compatibility regulations. Key types include Bulk Current Injection (BCI) for simulating radiated emissions, Conducted Emissions (CE) measuring unwanted electrical energy from cables, and Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE) assessing sudden voltage surges. Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI) tests resilience against voltage spikes, while Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) evaluates system robustness. Radiated Emissions (RE) assess airborne electromagnetic interference and Radiated Immunity (RI) tests vulnerability to external electromagnetic fields.</p></p>
