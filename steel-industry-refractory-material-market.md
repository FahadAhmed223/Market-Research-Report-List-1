<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel industry refractory material market is primarily divided into two types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories consist of pre-formed, durable bricks designed for high-temperature applications, offering excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength. In contrast, monolithic refractories are castable materials that can be easily shaped and applied to complex structures, ensuring seamless lining without joints. Both types are essential for furnaces, ladles, and other steelmaking equipment, providing insulation and protection against extreme heat and chemical exposure.</p></p>
