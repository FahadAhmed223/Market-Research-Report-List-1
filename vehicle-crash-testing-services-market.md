<p><p>Vehicle crash testing services evaluate vehicle safety through two main market types: **Passive Safety Testing** and **Active Safety Testing**. Passive Safety Testing focuses on assessing systems that protect occupants during a crash, such as airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones, ensuring minimal injury. Active Safety Testing evaluates technologies that prevent accidents, like anti-lock braking systems (ABS), electronic stability control (ESC), and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Both markets aim to enhance vehicle safety, with passive systems mitigating crash impact and active systems reducing collision risks.</p></p>
