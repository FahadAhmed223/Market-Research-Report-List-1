<p><p>The electrical explosion-proof equipment market comprises various types designed for hazardous environments. Flame-proof type enclosures prevent ignition through robust construction. Positive-pressure type uses pressurized barriers to keep hazardous gases out. Oil-immersed type isolates components in oil, reducing ignition risk. Sand filled type uses sand as a barrier against explosions. Other types may include hybrid or specialized solutions tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
