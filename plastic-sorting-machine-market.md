<p><strong>The Plastic Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Sorting Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Sorting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic sorting machine market includes two primary types: chute-type sorting machines and belt-type sorting machines. Chute-type machines utilize gravity to direct plastic materials through a series of chutes, where sensors identify and separate different plastics based on their properties. In contrast, belt-type sorting machines employ conveyor belts to transport materials while integrated sensors sort plastics using various methods such as infrared or optical recognition. Both systems are essential for enhancing recycling efficiency and improving the quality of sorted plastics.</p></p>
