<p><p>The stationary CT scanner market is categorized into high-slice, mid-slice, low-slice, and cone-beam CT (CBCT) systems. High-slice CT scanners, with more than 64 slices, provide detailed imaging and faster acquisition times, suited for complex diagnostics. Mid-slice CT, typically 16 to 64 slices, balances speed and resolution for routine examinations. Low-slice CT, often fewer than 16 slices, is used for basic imaging needs. CBCT technology specializes in 3D imaging, mainly in dental and orthopedic applications, offering low radiation doses for localized areas.</p></p>
