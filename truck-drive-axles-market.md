<p><p>Truck drive axles are crucial components in heavy-duty vehicles, categorized into three main types: single, tandem, and tridem. </p><p>Single axles support lighter loads, making them ideal for smaller trucks. Tandem axles, featuring two axles working in unison, enhance load capacity and stability, commonly found in medium to heavy trucks. Tridem axles, which include three axles, provide even greater load distribution and are often used in large trucks or trailers, ensuring efficient handling and better traction on challenging terrains.</p></p>
