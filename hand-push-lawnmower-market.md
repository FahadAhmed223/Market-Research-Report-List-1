<p><p>The hand push lawnmower market comprises three main types: petrol, electric, and manual lawnmowers. Petrol hand push lawnmowers are powerful and ideal for larger lawns, offering mobility without the need for electrical outlets. Electric hand push lawnmowers are quieter, environmentally friendly, and suitable for smaller to medium-sized lawns, often featuring corded or battery-operated models. Manual hand push lawnmowers, also known as reel mowers, are eco-friendly and require no fuel or electricity, appealing to those seeking a simple, low-maintenance solution for small lawns.</p></p>
