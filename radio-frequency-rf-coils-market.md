<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) Coils market is categorized primarily into volume coils and surface coils, used in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). Volume coils provide homogeneous signal detection over a larger area, making them suitable for whole-body imaging. In contrast, surface coils are designed for capturing signals from specific regions, enhancing resolution and signal-to-noise ratios for targeted imaging. This differentiation allows for tailored applications, improving diagnostic accuracy and adaptability in various medical imaging scenarios.</p></p>
