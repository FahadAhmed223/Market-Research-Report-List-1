<p><p>The articulated dump truck tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing a cushioned ride and improved traction, ideal for off-road conditions. Solid tires, made from a durable rubber compound, offer enhanced puncture resistance and are suitable for rough terrain but provide a harsher ride. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering durability and a smoother ride while resisting wear and tear, making them versatile for various applications in construction and mining sectors.</p></p>
