<p><p>The commercial aircraft landing gear market includes various types designed for specific operational needs. Strut landing gear, commonly used in jetliners, offers stability and support during landing and takeoff. Rocker landing gear features a pivoting mechanism, providing enhanced terrain adaptability, ideal for certain regional aircraft. Pontoon landing gear is specialized for floatplanes, allowing landings on water. Framed landing gear, often seen in smaller aircraft, combines lightweight construction with durability, catering to general aviation and short takeoff and landing needs.</p></p>
