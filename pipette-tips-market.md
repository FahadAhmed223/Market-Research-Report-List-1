<p><p>The pipette tips market is divided into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general liquid handling applications, providing accurate and precise measurements without any filtration, making them suitable for routine tasks. In contrast, filtered pipette tips include built-in barriers that prevent aerosols and contaminants from entering the pipette, ideal for sensitive applications involving volatile or infectious samples. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, enhancing accuracy and safety in various scientific workflows.</p></p>
