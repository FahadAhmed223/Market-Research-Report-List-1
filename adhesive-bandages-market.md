<p><p>The adhesive bandages market comprises various types, including flexible fabric bandages and cohesive fixation bandages. Flexible fabric bandages are designed for comfort and adaptability, allowing movement while providing protection to wounds. They are often made from breathable materials that promote healing. Cohesive fixation bandages, on the other hand, adhere to themselves without sticking to the skin, making them suitable for securing dressings or immobilizing injuries. Both types play essential roles in wound care and injury management.</p></p>
