<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Fork Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN</li><li>PNP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic fork sensor market is primarily divided into NPN and PNP types, representing different configurations of electronic output. NPN sensors provide a sinking output, where the load current flows through the sensor to ground, making them suitable for use in sourcing circuits. In contrast, PNP sensors offer a sourcing output, supplying current to the load when activated. Both types are crucial in industrial automation and material handling applications, providing precise detection capabilities based on the presence of objects.</p></p>
