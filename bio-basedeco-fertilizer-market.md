<p><p>The bio-based (eco) fertilizer market includes nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, potash-mobilizing, and other types. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers enhance soil nitrogen levels by converting atmospheric nitrogen into plant-usable forms. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers release bound phosphorus, improving nutrient availability. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers increase potassium uptake by breaking down soil minerals. Other types include biofertilizers with micronutrients or growth-promoting microbes. These eco-friendly alternatives reduce chemical dependency, improve soil health, and promote sustainable agriculture by leveraging natural processes for nutrient enrichment, making them vital for environmentally conscious farming practices.</p></p>
