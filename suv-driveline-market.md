<p><ul><li>Series Driveline</li><li>Parallel Driveline</li><li>Power Split Driveline</li><li>Electric Driveline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV driveline market encompasses various configurations tailored for performance and efficiency. Series driveline systems use one power source, typically a generator, supplying energy to electric motors. Parallel driveline systems combine internal combustion engines with electric motors, allowing for flexible propulsion. Power split driveline combines elements of both, enabling efficient power distribution between the engine and motors. Electric driveline systems rely solely on electric power, offering zero emissions and high efficiency. Each type targets diverse consumer needs and driving conditions.</p></p>
