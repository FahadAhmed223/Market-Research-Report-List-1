<p><p>Level monitoring float sensors are essential devices used to detect and monitor liquid levels in various applications. The two main types are top-mounted and side-mounted float sensors. Top-mounted sensors are installed above the liquid surface, using a buoyancy mechanism to detect changes in level and are ideal for tanks with limited access. Side-mounted sensors, on the other hand, are attached to the side of a tank or container, providing accurate level readings and are suitable for larger vessels with more space.</p></p>
