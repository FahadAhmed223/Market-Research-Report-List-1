<p><ul><li>Superconducting</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MR Neuro Imaging market consists of two primary types: superconducting and permanent magnet systems. Superconducting MRI systems utilize advanced superconductive materials, allowing for high magnetic fields and improved imaging resolution, often preferred in clinical settings due to their superior performance. In contrast, permanent magnet MRI systems use permanent magnets, offering lower operational costs and portability, making them suitable for outpatient facilities and smaller practices. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
