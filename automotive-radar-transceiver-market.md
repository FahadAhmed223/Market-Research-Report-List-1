<p><strong>The Automotive Radar Transceiver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BiCMOS</li><li>RFCMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive radar transceiver market is primarily divided into two types: BiCMOS and RFCMOS. BiCMOS technology combines bipolar and CMOS transistors, offering high performance and low power consumption, making it suitable for short-range applications. RFCMOS, on the other hand, utilizes CMOS technology optimized for RF applications, providing better integration and reduced costs for longer-range radar systems. Both technologies drive advancements in vehicle safety and automation, enhancing functionalities like adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance.</p></p>
