<p><p>Intraoral phosphor screen scanners are utilized in dental imaging, providing a digital alternative to traditional film. The market comprises two main types: pointer type and digital type. Pointer type scanners use a handheld device to position the phosphor plates and read images, offering portability and ease of use.
