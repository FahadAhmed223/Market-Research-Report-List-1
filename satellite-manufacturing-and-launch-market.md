<p><strong>The Satellite Manufacturing and Launch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LEO</li><li>GEO</li><li>MEO</li><li>Beyond GEO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Satellite manufacturing and launch markets are categorized based on the orbits they serve: </p><p>1. **LEO (Low Earth Orbit)**: Satellites in this orbit facilitate communication, Earth observation, and internet services, typically operating between 160 to 2,000 km above Earth.</p><p>2. **MEO (Medium Earth Orbit)**: Positioned between LEO and GEO, these satellites are primarily used for navigation systems like GPS, usually orbiting at 2,000 to 35,786 km.</p><p>3. **GEO (Geostationary Orbit)**: This orbit allows satellites to match Earth's rotation, ideal for weather monitoring and telecommunications at around 35,786 km.</p><p>4. **Beyond GEO**: This includes satellites designed for deep space missions and scientific exploration, operating beyond traditional geostationary limits.</p></p>
