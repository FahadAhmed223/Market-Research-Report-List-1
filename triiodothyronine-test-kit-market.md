<p><p>The Triiodothyronine Test Kit market encompasses various testing methodologies, primarily Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent labels for sensitive detection of triiodothyronine levels, allowing for rapid results with high specificity. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent labels that produce light as a response to hormonal presence, enabling quantification of T3 levels with enhanced sensitivity. Both methods facilitate accurate assessments of thyroid function, supporting diagnosis and management of related disorders.</p></p>
