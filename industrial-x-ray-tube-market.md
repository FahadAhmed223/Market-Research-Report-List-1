<p><ul><li>Stationary X-ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode X-ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Tube market consists of two main types: stationary X-ray tubes and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary X-ray tubes feature a fixed anode, making them suitable for applications requiring lower power and simpler designs, primarily in non-destructive testing. In contrast, rotating anode X-ray tubes have a rotating target, allowing for higher power output and faster imaging, making them ideal for more demanding applications such as industrial inspections and advanced imaging techniques.</p></p>
