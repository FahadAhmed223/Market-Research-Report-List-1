<p><p>The precision casting parts market primarily encompasses two types: Silica Sol Casting and Water Glass Casting. Silica Sol Casting utilizes a sol-gel process, offering high precision and excellent surface finish, making it ideal for intricate shapes and thin-walled components. In contrast, Water Glass Casting, also known as sodium silicate casting, employs a less expensive medium and is suitable for larger parts with lower precision requirements. Both methods cater to diverse industries such as aerospace, automotive, and machinery.</p></p>
