<p><ul><li>Analog Clip-on Extensometers</li><li>Digital Clip-on Extensometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clip-on extensometers market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog clip-on extensometers use mechanical systems to measure elongation and deformation, providing a continuous output via a dial gauge or similar device. These are typically user-friendly and cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, digital clip-on extensometers use electronic sensors to provide precise, real-time measurements displayed on digital screens. They often include advanced features like data logging and connectivity options, making them suitable for complex testing environments.</p></p>
