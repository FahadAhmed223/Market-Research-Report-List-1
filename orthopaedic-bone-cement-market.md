<p><p>The orthopaedic bone cement market comprises three primary types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are ideal for simple fractures and offer excellent flow for minimal invasiveness. Medium viscosity cements provide a balance between strength and workability, suitable for a range of applications. High viscosity cements are used for situations requiring enhanced stability and load-bearing capacity, specifically in complex surgeries or high-stress regions. Each type plays a critical role in improving patient outcomes in orthopaedic procedures.</p></p>
