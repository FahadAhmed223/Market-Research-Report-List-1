<p><p>Phase Change Materials (PCMs) are substances that absorb or release thermal energy during phase transitions, such as from solid to liquid and vice versa. The PCM market is categorized into three main types: organic, inorganic, and bio-based. Organic PCMs, derived from natural products or petroleum, are non-toxic and have high latent heat. Inorganic PCMs, typically salts or salt hydrates, offer high thermal conductivity and energy density. Bio-based PCMs are derived from renewable resources, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
