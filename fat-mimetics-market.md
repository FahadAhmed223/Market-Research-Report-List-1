<p><p>The fat mimetics market includes three main types: carbohydrate-based, protein-based, and lipid-based solutions. Carbohydrate-based mimetics use starches and fibers to create a creamy texture while reducing fat content. Protein-based mimetics incorporate proteins to enhance mouthfeel and satiety without the calories of traditional fats. Lipid-based mimetics simulate the properties of fats by using plant oils and other lipid sources to achieve similar taste and texture profiles in low-fat food products, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
