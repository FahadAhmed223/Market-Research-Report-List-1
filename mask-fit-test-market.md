<p><ul><li>Qualitative Test</li><li>Quantitative Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Fit Test market is divided into two main types: qualitative and quantitative tests. Qualitative tests assess mask fit based on the wearer’s subjective experience, often using techniques like the saccharin or Bitrex tests to determine if taste particles penetrate the mask. Quantitative tests, on the other hand, employ instruments to measure the actual leakage of air and particles around the mask seal, providing objective data on fit performance. Both types are essential for ensuring effective respiratory protection in various settings.</p></p>
