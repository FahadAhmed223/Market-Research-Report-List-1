<p><strong>The EVA Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal EVA</li><li>Anti-PID EVA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) film market includes **Normal EVA** and **Anti-PID EVA** types. Normal EVA is widely used in solar panels for encapsulation, offering excellent adhesion, durability, and light transmission. Anti-PID EVA, on the other hand, is designed to prevent Potential Induced Degradation (PID), a common issue in solar modules that reduces efficiency. Anti-PID EVA incorporates additives to resist electrical leakage, ensuring long-term performance and reliability, making it ideal for high-efficiency solar applications in demanding environments.</p></p>
