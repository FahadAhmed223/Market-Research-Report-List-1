<p><ul><li>SiO/C</li><li>Si/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon anode material market primarily includes two types: SiO/C (silicon oxide/carbon) and Si/C (silicon/carbon). SiO/C combines silicon oxide with carbon, enhancing the anode's stability and performance by mitigating volume expansion during charging cycles. Conversely, Si/C utilizes a blend of silicon and carbon to achieve high energy density while benefiting from carbon's conductivity. Both materials are integral to advancing lithium-ion batteries, improving their efficiency, capacity, and lifespan for applications in electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
