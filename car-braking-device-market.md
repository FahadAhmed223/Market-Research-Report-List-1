<p><strong>The Car Braking Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Box System</li><li>One-Box System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car braking device market includes two main types: the Two-Box System and the One-Box System. The Two-Box System separates the brake pressure generation (hydraulic unit) and the electronic control unit (ECU), offering flexibility and redundancy, often used in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). The One-Box System integrates both functions into a single unit, reducing size, weight, and cost while improving efficiency. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle safety and performance, with the choice depending on design priorities and application requirements.</p></p>
