<p><p>Focused Ultrasound Therapy Systems are categorized into two main types: Ultrasound-Guided and MRI-Guided. Ultrasound-Guided systems utilize real-time imaging to target tissues, allowing for precise delivery of ultrasound waves to the treatment area, often preferred for cost-effectiveness and portability. In contrast, MRI-Guided systems leverage magnetic resonance imaging to provide highly detailed visuals of the target, ensuring greater accuracy in treatment. Both types aim to non-invasively treat various medical conditions by selectively destroying tissue without harming surrounding areas.</p></p>
