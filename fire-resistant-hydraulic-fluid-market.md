<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fire-Resistant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing, providing high fire resistance but lower lubricating properties. HFB fluids are non-water-containing and exhibit better lubricity but may still be flammable. HFC fluids are water-containing synthetic fluids with excellent fire resistance and superior performance. HFD fluids are non-water-containing synthetic materials, offering high temperatures and reduced flammability, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
