<p><p>The disposable syringes polypropylene market is primarily segmented into two types: PP-H (homopolymer) and PP-R (random copolymer). PP-H offers high clarity and rigidity, making it suitable for applications that require strength and visibility. In contrast, PP-R provides better impact resistance and flexibility, ideal for applications needing durability under stress. Both types are widely used in medical settings for their chemical resistance and safety features, facilitating efficient and hygienic administration of medications.</p></p>
