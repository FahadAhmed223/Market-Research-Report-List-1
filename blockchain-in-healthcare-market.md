<p><ul><li>Public Blockchain</li><li>Private Blockchain</li><li>Permissioned Blockchain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blockchain in healthcare market comprises three main types: public, private, and permissioned blockchains. Public blockchains are open networks that allow anyone to participate, promoting transparency and decentralization. Private blockchains are restricted to specific users, ensuring confidentiality and control over access, making them suitable for sensitive health data. Permissioned blockchains combine elements of both, where only authorized participants can access the network, offering a balance between security and collaboration, often used for regulatory compliance and data sharing among trusted entities.</p></p>
