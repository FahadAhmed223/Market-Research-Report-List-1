<p><p>The Multi-Head Powder Filling Machine market primarily includes two types: spiral and linear. Spiral filling machines utilize a rotating spiral mechanism, allowing for efficient and continuous powder dispensing, ideal for high-speed production environments. Linear filling machines operate on a straight-line system, filling containers sequentially, which suits smaller batch operations or varying container sizes. Both types enhance accuracy and speed in powder packaging, catering to diverse industry needs, from pharmaceuticals to food and cosmetics.</p></p>
