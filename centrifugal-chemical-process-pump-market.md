<p><strong>The Centrifugal Chemical Process Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End Suction</li><li>Back Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal chemical process pump market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into end suction and back pullout pumps. End suction pumps feature a design where the fluid enters the pump from one end, making them suitable for various applications due to their compactness and efficiency. Back pullout pumps, on the other hand, allow for easy maintenance since the rotating assembly can be removed without disconnecting the piping, offering convenience for chemical handling in industrial processes. Both designs ensure effective fluid movement and reliability.</p></p>
