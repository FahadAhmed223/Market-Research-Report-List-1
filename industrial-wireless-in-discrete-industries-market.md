<p><p>Industrial wireless in discrete industries encompasses various communication technologies tailored for specific applications. Wireless Local Area Networks (WLAN) facilitate high-speed connectivity within a limited area, ideal for real-time data transfer. Wireless Personal Area Networks (WPAN) connect devices over short distances, suitable for sensor networks. Satellite (GNSS) provides global positioning and communication coverage, crucial for remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Networks (LPWAN) enable long-range communication with minimal power consumption, perfect for IoT devices. Cellular networks offer robust coverage for mobile connectivity across extensive areas.</p></p>
