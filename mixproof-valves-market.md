<p><p>Mixproof valves are essential in industries where product integrity is crucial, such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. Two primary types of actuation are pneumatic and hydraulic. Pneumatic mixproof valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering quick response times and easy integration with automated systems. In contrast, hydraulic mixproof valves use fluid pressure to function, providing high force and precise control for larger applications. Both types ensure product segregation while maintaining hygiene and efficiency in processing.</p></p>
