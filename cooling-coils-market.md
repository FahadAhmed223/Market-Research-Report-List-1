<p><p>The cooling coils market is segmented into three main types: water cooling coils, steam cooling coils, and refrigerant cooling coils. Water cooling coils utilize chilled water to absorb heat, making them efficient for larger systems. Steam cooling coils use steam to transfer heat, providing rapid temperature control. Meanwhile, refrigerant cooling coils rely on refrigerants to absorb and release heat, commonly found in air conditioning systems. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various industrial and commercial cooling needs.</p></p>
