<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resonant</li><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Optical</li><li>Others (potentiometric, piezoelectric, and thermal technologies)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absolute pressure sensors market comprises various technology types. Piezoresistive sensors utilize changes in resistance due to pressure, while capacitive sensors measure variations in capacitance. Resonant sensors rely on frequency changes in vibrating elements. Electromagnetic sensors detect pressure through magnetic field variations, and optical sensors utilize light changes for measurement. Additionally, other technologies include potentiometric sensors, which measure voltage changes, piezoelectric sensors generating electrical charges from mechanical stress, and thermal sensors that assess pressure through heat transfer variations.</p></p>
