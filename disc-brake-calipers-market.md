<p><p>The disc brake caliper market is primarily divided into two types: plug type and rotating type. Plug-type calipers feature a straightforward design where the piston moves linearly to clamp the brake pads, offering effective braking performance. In contrast, rotating-type calipers utilize a pivot point, allowing the caliper to rotate as the brake pedal is pressed, resulting in enhanced force distribution and improved braking efficiency. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and performance standards within the automotive industry.</p></p>
