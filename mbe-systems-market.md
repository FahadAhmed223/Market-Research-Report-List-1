<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The MBE Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal MBE Systems</li><li>Laser MBE Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MBE (Molecular Beam Epitaxy) systems are categorized into Normal MBE Systems and Laser MBE Systems. Normal MBE systems utilize traditional effusion cells to deposit materials onto substrates in a vacuum, ensuring precise control over thickness and composition. In contrast, Laser MBE systems incorporate lasers to enhance the deposition process, allowing for better temperature control and improved material quality. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor fabrication, enabling the production of high-performance materials for electronics and optoelectronics applications.</p></p>
