<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polybutylene Terephthalate (PBT) market is primarily categorized into two key processing types: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves shaping the fiberglass-reinforced PBT by injecting it into molds, allowing for complex geometries and high precision in parts like automotive components and electronics. In contrast, extrusion molding produces continuous shapes by forcing the material through a die, suitable for applications such as pipes and sheets. Both methods enhance the mechanical strength and durability of PBT, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
