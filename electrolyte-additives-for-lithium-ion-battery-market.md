Fluorinated Ethylene Carbonate (FEC) enhances the thermal stability and cycling performance. 1,3-Propane Sultone (1,3-PS) promotes stable electrolyte interfaces. Vinyl Ethylene Carbonate (VEC) contributes to improved capacity retention. Lithium Bis(fluorosulfonyl)imide (LiFSI) enhances ionic conductivity and battery safety. Other additives may include various compounds tailored for specific performance metrics in different battery applications.</p></p>
