<p><ul><li>UV-Visible Detectors</li><li>Refractive Index Detectors</li><li>ELSD Detectors</li><li>Fluorescence Detectors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Wavelength Detectors market encompasses several types, each catering to specific analytical needs. UV-Visible Detectors measure absorbance across a range of wavelengths, essential for identifying compounds. Refractive Index Detectors determine changes in light refraction, useful for non-chromophoric substances. ELSD Detectors detect analytes by measuring light scattering, advantageous for non-UV-active compounds. Fluorescence Detectors utilize the emission of light from excited molecules, providing high sensitivity. Other detectors include mass spectrometry and conductivity detectors, offering diverse functionalities for various applications.</p></p>
