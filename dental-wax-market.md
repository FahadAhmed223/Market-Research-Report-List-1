<p><p>The dental wax market features various types including pattern wax, processing wax, impression wax, and healing wax. Pattern wax is used to create accurate shapes and models in dental prosthetics. Processing wax aids in temporary fixes and adjustments during dental procedures. Impression wax captures detailed impressions of teeth and gums for diagnostics and treatment planning. Healing wax is applied to soothe and protect oral tissues post-treatment, enhancing patient comfort and promoting recovery. Each type plays a vital role in modern dentistry.</p></p>
