<p><p>The natural gas-powered truck market primarily comprises CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) trucks and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) trucks. CNG trucks use compressed natural gas stored in high-pressure tanks, suitable for light to medium-duty applications, offering lower emissions and operational costs. In contrast, LNG trucks utilize liquefied natural gas, which is stored at cryogenic temperatures, making them ideal for long-haul trucking due to a higher energy density and extended range. Both types contribute to sustainability in transportation.</p></p>
