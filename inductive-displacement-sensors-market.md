<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Inductive Type</li><li>Mutual Inductive Type</li><li>Eddy Current Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive displacement sensors are categorized into three main types in the market. Self-inductive types utilize changes in inductance caused by the movement of an object near the sensor, enabling precise position measurements. Mutual inductive types involve two coils, where the displacement of the target affects the mutual inductance between them, allowing for accurate detection. Eddy current types operate based on the induced eddy currents in conductive materials, providing non-contact measurement capabilities. Each type offers distinct benefits for various industrial applications.</p></p>
