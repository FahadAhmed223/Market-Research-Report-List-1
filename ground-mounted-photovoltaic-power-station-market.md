<p><p>The ground-mounted photovoltaic power station market features two primary types: crystalline silicon and thin film photovoltaic power stations. Crystalline silicon power stations utilize silicon-based solar cells, known for high efficiency and durability, making them the most common type. In contrast, thin film photovoltaic power stations employ flexible and lightweight materials, offering versatility in installation and lower production costs, though generally with reduced efficiency. Both types contribute significantly to renewable energy generation, catering to diverse energy needs and deployment scenarios.</p></p>
