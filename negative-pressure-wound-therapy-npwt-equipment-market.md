<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) Equipment is a medical device used to facilitate wound healing by applying sub-atmospheric pressure to the wound site. This therapy involves sealed dressings connected to a vacuum pump that removes excess exudate, reduces edema, and enhances blood flow, thereby promoting tissue granulation and closure. It is widely utilized in chronic wounds, surgical wounds, and traumatic injuries, significantly improving healing outcomes.</p><p>The NPWT Equipment market is projected to experience notable growth, with an expected CAGR of 3.60% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving this growth, including the increasing prevalence of chronic wounds, rise in surgical procedures, and the growing geriatric population prone to various health issues. Additionally, advancements in NPWT technology, such as portable and lightweight devices, are attracting more healthcare providers and enhancing patient comfort.</p><p>Emerging trends include the integration of NPWT with advanced wound care solutions and a focus on home healthcare settings, where patients can continue therapy outside the hospital.
