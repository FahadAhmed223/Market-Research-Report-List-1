<p><p>The sand aluminum casting market primarily consists of two types: green sand and dry sand. Green sand, a mixture of sand, clay, and water, is widely used for its excellent ability to retain moisture and mold shapes effectively. It's cost-effective and suitable for high-volume production. Dry sand, on the other hand, lacks moisture and offers greater strength and detail in casting, making it ideal for intricate designs and high-quality finishes. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs in the aluminum casting industry.</p></p>
