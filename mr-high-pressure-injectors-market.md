<p><p>The MR High Pressure Injectors market is categorized into two main types: Single Head and Dual Head injectors. Single Head MR High Pressure Injectors are designed to deliver contrast media through a single injection point, making them ideal for simpler procedures with less complexity. In contrast, Dual Head MR High Pressure Injectors feature two independent injection chambers, allowing simultaneous delivery and enhanced efficiency for more complex imaging procedures. Both types cater to the varying demands of medical imaging and diagnostics.</p></p>
