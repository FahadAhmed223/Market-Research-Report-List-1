<p><ul><li>Machine Mill Flour</li><li>Stone Mill Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Home Organic Bread Flour market is primarily divided into two types: machine mill flour and stone mill flour. Machine mill flour is produced using modern industrial processes that yield a consistent texture and fine grain, ideal for regular baking needs. In contrast, stone mill flour is made by grinding grains between two stones, preserving more nutrients and providing a coarser texture, which appeals to health-conscious consumers seeking artisanal quality. Both types cater to the growing demand for organic baking ingredients.</p></p>
