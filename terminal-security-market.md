<p><p>The terminal security market is primarily divided into two key types: Endpoint Protection Platforms (EPP) and Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR). EPP focuses on preventing malware and other threats through antivirus tools, application control, and firewall features, providing a first layer of defense. In contrast, EDR emphasizes the detection of ongoing threats and response capabilities, utilizing advanced analytics and monitoring to identify suspicious activities. Together, these solutions enhance overall endpoint security, protecting devices from diverse cyber threats.</p></p>
