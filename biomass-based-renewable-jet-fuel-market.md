<p><p>The biomass-based renewable jet fuel market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene)** utilizes oils and fats for jet fuel production. </p><p>2. **FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene)** converts biomass into synthetic fuels via gasification and catalysis. </p><p>3. **ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene)** involves converting alcohols derived from biomass into jet fuel. </p><p>4. **Co-processing** blends renewable feedstocks with traditional fossil fuels in existing refineries. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass emerging technologies and feedstocks, contributing to the evolving aviation biofuel landscape.</p></p>
