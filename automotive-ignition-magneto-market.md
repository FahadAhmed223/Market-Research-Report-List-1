<p><p>The automotive ignition magneto market includes two primary types: self-contained systems and battery-powered systems. Self-contained systems generate electrical energy through mechanical means, typically using the engine's movement, making them independent of an external power source. This makes them reliable in various conditions. In contrast, battery-powered systems rely on a dedicated battery to provide the necessary voltage for ignition, offering consistent performance and easier starting. Each system has its advantages, catering to different vehicle types and user preferences.</p></p>
