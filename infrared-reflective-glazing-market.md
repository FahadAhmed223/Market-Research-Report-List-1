<p><p>The infrared reflective glazing (IRR glazing) market consists of two main types: Direct-Coat IRR Glazing and IRR Glazing with IR Film. Direct-Coat IRR Glazing involves a specialized coating applied directly to the glass surface, enhancing heat rejection while maintaining clarity. On the other hand, IRR Glazing with IR Film incorporates an additional film layer that reflects infrared radiation, further improving energy efficiency and comfort. Both types aim to optimize thermal performance in buildings, contributing to sustainability and energy savings.</p></p>
