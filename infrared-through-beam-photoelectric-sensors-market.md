<p><p>Infrared Through Beam Photoelectric Sensors are crucial in detecting objects and controlling automated processes. The market for these sensors is divided into two main types based on signal output: Digital Signal and Analog Signal. Digital Signal sensors provide a binary output, indicating the presence or absence of an object, which is ideal for straightforward on/off applications. In contrast, Analog Signal sensors output a continuous range of values, allowing for more precise measurements of distance or position, suited for complex detection tasks.</p></p>
