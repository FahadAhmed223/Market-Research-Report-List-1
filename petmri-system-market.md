<p><p>The PET/MRI system market is categorized into three types: fully integrated systems, separate devices, and pulse oximetry screening solutions. Fully integrated systems combine positron emission tomography (PET) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) into a single unit, optimizing workflow and image quality. Separate devices consist of standalone PET and MRI machines, offering flexibility in usage. Pulse oximetry screening complements these imaging modalities by providing vital sign information, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient monitoring during imaging procedures.</p></p>
