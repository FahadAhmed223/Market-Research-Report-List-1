<p><p>The immersion suits market is categorized into insulated and un-insulated suits. Insulated immersion suits are designed to provide thermal protection in cold water, maintaining body heat for extended periods and ensuring survival in harsh conditions. They are ideal for cold climates and scenarios where prolonged exposure to frigid waters occurs.
