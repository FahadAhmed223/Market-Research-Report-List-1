<p><p>Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT) sensors are precise instruments used for measuring linear displacement. <p><p>The Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT) sensor market is primarily divided into two types: DC-operated and AC-operated LVDT sensors. DC-operated LVDTs are powered by a direct current supply, offering high precision and low noise, making them suitable for applications requiring stable readings in challenging environments. In contrast, AC-operated LVDTs utilize alternating current, featuring higher sensitivity and robust performance over longer distances. Both types are widely used in industrial automation, aerospace, and test and measurement applications, catering to diverse sensing needs.</p></p>
