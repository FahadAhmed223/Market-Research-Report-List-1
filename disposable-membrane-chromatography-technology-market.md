<p><p>Disposable membrane chromatography technology encompasses various market types, including ion exchange membrane chromatography, affinity membrane chromatography, and hydrophobic interaction membrane chromatography. Ion exchange membrane chromatography focuses on separating biomolecules based on charge, making it ideal for protein purification. Affinity membrane chromatography utilizes specific interactions between ligands and targets to isolate proteins or antibodies with high selectivity. Hydrophobic interaction membrane chromatography exploits hydrophobic interactions for separating proteins based on their hydrophobicity, providing a versatile tool for purifying complex biomolecular mixtures.</p></p>
