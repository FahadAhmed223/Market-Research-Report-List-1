<p><strong>The 3D Printing Technology in Pharmaceutical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Material Extrusion</li><li>Binder Jetting</li><li>Powder Bed Fusion</li><li>VAT Photopolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D printing technology in the pharmaceutical market employs various methods to create customized drug delivery systems and implants. Material extrusion involves layering thermoplastic materials to form drug-filled matrices. Binder jetting utilizes a binding agent to consolidate powder particles, enabling the creation of complex drug structures. Powder bed fusion melts powdered materials to form solid forms, ensuring precise dosing. VAT photopolymerization uses light to cure liquid resins into solid forms, allowing intricate designs for tailored pharmaceuticals and medical devices.</p></p>
