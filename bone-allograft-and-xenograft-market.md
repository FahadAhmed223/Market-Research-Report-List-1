<p><p>The bone allograft and xenograft market consists of two primary types: allografts and xenografts. Allografts involve the transplantation of bone tissue from one human donor to another, typically used for orthopedic and dental procedures. Xenografts, on the other hand, utilize bone tissue from a different species, often bovine, to serve as a scaffold for bone regeneration. Both types are crucial for enhancing surgical outcomes, promoting healing, and providing viable options for patients requiring reconstructive surgeries.</p></p>
