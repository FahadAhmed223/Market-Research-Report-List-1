<p><p>The disposable medical sterile surgical gloves market consists of two primary types: natural latex surgical gloves and synthetic surgical gloves. Natural latex gloves, made from rubber tree latex, offer excellent elasticity and comfort but can cause allergic reactions in some users. Synthetic surgical gloves, often manufactured from materials like nitrile or vinyl, provide alternatives for individuals with latex allergies and offer enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and safety in medical and surgical environments.</p></p>
