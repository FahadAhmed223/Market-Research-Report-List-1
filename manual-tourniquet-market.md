<p><strong>The Manual Tourniquet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Latex Tourniquet</li><li>TPE Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual tourniquet market includes two primary types: latex and TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditional, known for their elasticity and effectiveness in controlling bleeding during medical procedures. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some patients. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, offer a hypoallergenic alternative, providing similar functionality with increased durability and comfort. Both types are essential in emergency settings and surgeries, catering to varying patient sensitivities and procedural requirements.</p></p>
