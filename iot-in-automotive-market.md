<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) in the automotive market encompasses three key types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. Embedded refers to built-in connectivity within the vehicle, enabling real-time data exchange and advanced functionality. Tethered involves using smartphones or other portable devices to connect with the vehicle, allowing access to apps and features. Integrated combines both embedded and tethered approaches, enhancing user experience by seamlessly linking vehicle systems with external devices and cloud services, thus improving safety, navigation, and maintenance.</p></p>
