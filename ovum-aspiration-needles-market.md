<p><p>Ovum aspiration needles are essential tools in assisted reproductive technology, used to retrieve oocytes from ovarian follicles. The two main market types are single lumen and double-lumen needles. Single lumen needles have a single channel, allowing for straightforward aspiration of oocytes. In contrast, double-lumen needles feature two channels, facilitating simultaneous aspiration and flushing, which can enhance retrieval efficiency. Both types are designed to minimize discomfort and maximize oocyte yield, catering to varying clinical needs in fertility treatments.</p></p>
